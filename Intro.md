# 1. Product Description (Simple Explanation)

PushPilot is an AI-powered multi-channel notification automation platform that helps businesses generate, schedule, and send personalized notifications across app, web, SMS, WhatsApp, email, and other channels.

Users describe their business goals in natural language, and PushPilot automatically creates optimized campaigns, manages scheduling, and connects to Firebase for real-time messaging delivery.

---

# 2. Purpose / Problem It Solves

PushPilot solves these key problems:

## Business Problems

- Difficult and time-consuming to create effective push notifications manually.
- Developers/companies struggle to personalize notifications at scale.
- Managing campaigns across multiple channels (app/web/SMS/WhatsApp/email) is complex.
- Scheduling, tracking, and optimizing notifications requires multiple tools.
- Repetitive tasks like writing, planning, and segmenting notifications reduce productivity.

## PushPilot’s Solution

- Uses AI to auto-generate notification content based on prompts.
- Simplifies campaign creation with a unified dashboard.
- Provides templates, sample notifications, and automated suggestions.
- Integrated with Firebase for secure, real-time push delivery.
- Offers analytics like total notifications sent, schedule, progress, active/past campaigns.
